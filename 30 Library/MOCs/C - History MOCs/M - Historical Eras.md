# [[M - Historical Eras]]

The history of Europe is divided into four time periods:
- [[Prehistoric Era]] (before 800 BC)
- [[Classical Antiquity]] (800 BC to 500 AD)
- [[Middle Ages]] (500-1500 AD)
- [[Modern Era]] (since 1500 AD)
